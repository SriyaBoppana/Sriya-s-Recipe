Recipe for Choclate Pancakes

Follow the steps to have a lovely Choclate pancake

1.Get pancake flour,eggs,milk,and choclate chips

2.Mix the pancake flour with the egss and milk until its smooth and a liquad

3.Once mixed, add chocolate chips!

4.Put the pancake mix on the pan and turn them every 10 seconds until soft

5.Then Eat!
